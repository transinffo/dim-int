VADEVTRADE

VADEVTRADE was founded in 2017. Our company is focused on the international wholesale of industrial goods that are widely used in the production of final products. VADEVTRADE started as a supplier of consumables and equipment for the production of all types of digital large format advertising printing. Developing relationships with its customers and based on market needs, the Company is constantly expanding the range of supplied goods.

Direct contracts

Direct contracts with many manufacturers and suppliers in Southeast Asia and Europe, as well as direct contacts with leading companies - distributors / dealers allowed us to achieve significant results in a relatively short time. Our company is focused on promoting the products of leading Asian and European manufacturers, whose interests we represent.

Industrial supplies

Today the company's business is represented by a wide range of products, such as modern models of solvent, eco-solvent and UV printers, manufactured by LIYU and FLORA. This is the widest range of consumables (banner, self-adhesive films, canvas for printing), produced by GLP and YUAN YUAN, etc., as well as a wide range of paints from Asian and European manufacturers. In parallel with the development of the main segment of the sale of advertising materials, in 2019 the company opened a new direction of the wholesale trade in metal. We are constantly working on expanding the range of products and expanding our trade contacts.

Our mission

The goal of our activity in all markets where we operate is to provide our customers with the most interesting, functional, proven, and fundamentally new product models at the most affordable prices. The task of the VADEVTRADE Company is to offer our clients not only a wide choice, but also a complex of ready-made solutions that make cooperation mutually beneficial and most effective. We understand that the success of our business is based on the success of our clients' business, therefore, accessible, constant and trusting feedback from clients is an important tool for ensuring our effective work in the market. For each product, we strive to be market leaders in three aspects: price, novelty, availability (availability) of goods.

Partnership

Our clients are large trading companies-distributors / dealers with a reputation in the market and an extensive trading network, successfully developing their business. We use an individual approach when working with each client. Based on a detailed study of the markets, we are constantly working to attract new partners, expanding the circle of business communication and cooperation.

our team

The company’s employees are highly qualified specialists with the necessary knowledge to provide customers.
Knowledge of products, the search for an attractive solution and the assortment of goods necessary for the client, a flexible pricing policy – these are qualities that allow our employees to find a positive response from our clients and partners.
Our staff will always offer, in the shortest possible time, the solutions to the clients they need. We are always ready to offer you products from leading manufacturers at the required quality level, the required configuration and a wide range of accessories and an attractive price.
We regularly update the range of products we offer, for which purpose, our staff attends thematic exhibitions, monitors global development trends and provides up-to-date information on the selection and use of products from our range of products.
We are also ready to provide you with an expert assessment of your business and range of products, as well as assist in organizing the logistics of the products supplied by us at affordable, competitive prices.
We invite you to cooperation.
